What is the Mehfil
Not a concert — a gathering. Poetry, sound and silence, shared in one warm room.

Mirza Ki Mehfil is an intimate live ritual: a draped shawl, a single mic, a circle of listeners on the floor. It is the old idea of the mehfil — an evening of soul — brought back for a new room of people.
